Core Requirements

What it takes to secure your blue checkmark.

Press & Media Presence

Your profile must be notable. We help curate and identify significant media coverage from reputable sources to build your case.

Authenticity

Must represent a real person, registered business, or entity. Government-issued ID or official documentation is required for all applications.

Completeness

Your account must be public and have a bio, profile photo, and at least one post.

Uniqueness

Your account must be the unique presence of the person or business it represents. Only one account per person or business may be verified.

The Verification Journey

A white-glove approach to your digital legacy.

01

Eligibility Audit

We perform a deep-dive analysis of your current digital footprint, searching for press gaps, impersonation risks, and profile deficiencies that could lead to a rejection.

02

Profile Optimization

Our team handles the "packaging" of your profile—optimizing bios, links, and cross-platform consistency to meet the exact aesthetic and technical preferences of platform moderators.

03

Direct Submission

Utilizing our agency portals, we submit your application directly to our contacts at major platforms. This ensures your case is reviewed by a human expert rather than an automated algorithm.

04

Ongoing Monitoring

Once verified, we provide guidance on maintaining your status. We monitor for impersonators and manage the takedown of fake accounts to protect your blue checkmark.
